Butterworth low-pass filter, fourth order.

Starting from analogue prototype transfer functions, using Tustin (bilinear) transform to arrive at digital approximation.

Butterworth filters are 'maximally flat' before cut-off frequency, i.e. in passband.

Gain of any order Butterworth low-pass filter at cut-off frequency is -3dB (1/sqrt(2)).

However, attenuation slope is n * (-20dB/dec), where n is the filter order.
